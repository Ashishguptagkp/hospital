Best Orthopedics Hospital in Gorakhpur

Orthopedics is a branch of medicine that deals with issues relating to the human musculoskeletal system. The orthopedic surgeon specializes in bones, joints, ligaments, tendons, and muscles - all parts of the musculoskeletal system that are necessary for everyday motion.

Orthopedists carry out these procedures

  • Muscle Strains
  • Carpal Uunnel Syndrome
  • Bone Fractures
  • Arthritis
  • Injuries to Tendons and Ligaments
  • Shoulder Pains and Problems
  • Joint and Back Pain
  • Limb Abnormalities
  • Bone Cancer
