Best General Surgery Hospital in Chintal Hyderabad

General surgery is a surgical specialty, which focuses on alimentary canal and abdominal contents including stomach, small and large intestines, liver, pancreas, appendix, etc. At RNC, our department of general surgery offers surgical interventions for all types of complicated health issues. Depending on the diagnosis, our highly experienced surgeons can offer best surgical options for our patients. Our highly qualified surgeons, operating room nurses, and associated health experts all play a vital role in the preoperative and postoperative evaluations and in taking care of the patient in the best way possible.

The department provides the following services:

  • Alimentary tract
  • Abdomen and its contents
  • Breast, skin, and soft tissue
  • Endocrine system
  • Simple and complex digestive diseases

In addition to routine surgical procedures, Dr. Bhaskar Mall Hospitals General Surgery Department also provides surgical treatment for a variety of health conditions that include appendicitis , hernias, and inguinal hernia. In addition to performing challenging and complicated surgeries, we have experienced surgeons who can treat life-threatening conditions in the abdomen, neck, liver, intestines, and chest. The general surgery department offers several surgical procedures on a short-term basis. An outpatient procedure can often be completed in one day, and the patient can return home the same day.
