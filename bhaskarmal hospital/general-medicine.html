Best General Medicine Hospital in Chintal Hyderabad

The practice of general or internal medicine involves treating patients holistically for all of their medical problems. Physicians on our staff have experience managing a broad range of medical problems, both emergencies and non-emergences. The department of General Medicine at RNC hospital provides comprehensive general health care and is staffed by highly qualified physicians.

The department provides the following services:

  • O.P.D
  • Speciality Clinic
  • I.P.D
  • Treatment and management of hypertension
  • Treatment and management of diabetes

We care for patients with serious medical problems in our high dependency unit and also offer our services in our intensive care unit. They are also experienced in managing lung-related issues and other diabetes-related problems. As part of their treatment process, our doctors follow national and international guidelines based on evidence-based medicine. The team at RNC hospital treats every patient's problem with the same passion and commitment, no matter if it is a common cold or something more serious.
