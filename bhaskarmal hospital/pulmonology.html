Best Pulmonology Hospital in Chintal Hyderabad

Pulmonology is the branch of medicine concerned with the health of the respiratory system. Asthma, tuberculosis, and other lung diseases are treated by pulmonologists. The respiratory system includes the organs that allow you to breathe. A lung, an airway, and respiratory muscles form the core of this system. They diagnose and treat conditions affecting men, women, and children's respiratory systems.

Procedures Performed by Pulmonologists are:

  • Asthma
  • Bronchitis
  • Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease
  • Emphysema
  • Interstitial Lung Disease
  • Obstructive Sleep Apnea
